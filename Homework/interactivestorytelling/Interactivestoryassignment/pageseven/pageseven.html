Sir Gawain of New Rapport

Sir Gawain tried in vain to get his horse back under control but in the mist of doing so his horse spins around but when it does, his lance slams right into the stomach of yet another guard! A loud clanging sound could be heard, as one of the guards threw a large stone at Sir Gawain and hit his helm so hard that he knocked it off of his head! Sir Gawain was knocked unconscious and was spread out across the back of his horse, as it being spooked by the sound of the helm being hit ran off at a full sprint up the road and disappeared into the maze of buildings leaving nothing behind but a dented helm which slid to a stop right in front of the Captain! He spots the Mayor's son, who had been hiding behind one of the rain barrels along side of the road, getting ready to run for help but he simply walked up behind him and brought the helm down upon the unsuspecting young man's head! The young man fell down to the ground with a thud, as the Captain looked the helm over curiously and saw something on the inside of the helm in bold print that caught his eye "Property of New Rapport Library". The sound of heavy footsteps caught his attention, as he quickly hid the helm behind some nearby boxes. The Mayor was leading the people down the road toward the starting point of the trial by ordeal but was awestruck to find all the guards that were escorting the Captain spread out all over the road some writhing in pain others laying there unconscious. The Mayor stopped short when he saw his son laying unconscious on the ground beside one of the rain barrels along the side of the road. He rushed to his son, cradling his head close against his chest, as his angry eyes turned to the Captain. The Mayor yelled at the Captain demanding how could he have done such a thing not only to his son but to the other guards that were escorting him.
