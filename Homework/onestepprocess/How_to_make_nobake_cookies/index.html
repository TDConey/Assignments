How to make No Bake Cookies

Ingredients

    2 cups of white sugar
     1/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
     1/2 cup of milk
     1/2 cup of margarine or butter
     1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
     1 pinch of salt
     1/2 cup of chunky peanut butter
     3 cups of quick cooking oats
     1 cup of sweetened flaked coconut

The amount of time used to make the No Bake Cookies

Preparation Time:

15 minutes

Cooking Time:

1 minute

Will be ready to eat in:

1 hour and 20 minutes

Cooking Instructions:

    In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar, cocoa, milk and margarine (or butter). Bring to it to a boil, stirring it occasionally. Boil for 1 minute, then remove from heat and stir in the vanilla, salt, peanut butter, coconut, and oats.
    Scoop the mixture and drop by rounded spoonfuls onto waxed paper. Allow cookies to cool for at least 1 hour. Store in an airtight container.
